Revenue ≠ profit, and Shopify only shows revenue
Your dashboard shows gross sales. It does not subtract the 2% transaction fee on Basic, the 2.9% + 30¢ Stripe takes, or the gap between what you charged and what your carrier actually billed you.
02
Ad spend looks fine in aggregate, deadly per order
A 3× ROAS sounds healthy. But split across individual orders, many brands spend $8–20 acquiring each customer — wiping out margin on the first purchase entirely.
03
Tax season is when merchants learn the truth
Most small D2C owners discover their real numbers in April. The $5K–10K surprise is almost always fees and COGS that were never tracked per order. By then, the damage is done.
